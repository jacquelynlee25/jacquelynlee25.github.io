Remix Project

The concept for my remix is how pollution and urban environments are taking over the natural beauty of the Earth. I used images of buildings to create the mountains while the reflection of how it should look is shown in the clear water. I also used polluted skies above the horizon to compare with the reflection of the actual sky.
