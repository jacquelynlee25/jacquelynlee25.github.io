Sound Project

Sounds of Daily Life from Jacquelyn Lee on Vimeo.

I wanted to use sounds people were familiar with and decided to record sound people hear in their daily lives. I purposely made them start at different times so that the listener can try to listen for each sound as it starts and finishes. It's best to listen with earphones because I tried to make each noise sound different in each ear.
